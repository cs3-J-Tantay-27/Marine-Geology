As the successor to the ODP, the IODP focused on scientific drilling of the ocean floor to understand Earth's history and structure. Key projects included Chikyu Drilling, which explored subduction zones, and Expedition 364, which studied the Chicxulub impact crater to understand the effects of the asteroid impact linked to the extinction of the dinosaurs.
